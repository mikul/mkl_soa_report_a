Customer Statement of Account

The Customer Statement of Account module provides a comprehensive solution for generating detailed account statements for your customers. It is designed to streamline and automate the process of tracking customer transactions, improving transparency and customer relationships.

Key Features

  • Generate customer account statements with ease.
  • Select a customizable date range for transactions.
  • Display opening balances, invoiced amounts, payments, and balances due.
  • Detailed transaction summary with amounts and balances.
  • Multi-currency and regional formatting for currency symbols.

Benefits

By using this module, businesses can:

  • Enhance customer satisfaction with clear and transparent statements.
  • Reduce manual work with automated data fetching and computation.
  • Ensure accuracy in financial reporting and reconciliation.

How It Works

The module integrates seamlessly into your Odoo platform. After installation:

  1. Navigate to the customer records.
  2. Click the "Print Statement of Account" button.
  3. Choose a date range and generate the report.

The generated PDF report includes:

  • Customer details (name, address).
  • Account summary with opening balances, payments, and balances due.
  • Detailed transaction history with dates, references, and amounts.

Compatibility

Compatible with Odoo 18 and supports all localization and currency formats.
